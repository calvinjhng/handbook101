Handbook 101

The Handbook for tech beginners

Get Started

No prior knowledge assumed

Each topic in Handbook 101 was written for the absolute beginner. No prior knowledge assumed!

Stand on the shoulders of giants

There are thousands of talented and highly competent instructors all over the world. Handbook 101 does not aim to re-invent the wheel. Instead, we seek to build on their excellent work and contribute value by organising that awesome information into a logical learning process.

Focus on Fundamentals

Give a man a fish and feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish and feed him for life. Handbook 101 aims to share the basic building blocks so that learners become confident and capable of learning on their own.
